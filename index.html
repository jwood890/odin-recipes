Cheeseburger Delight

Description

This is a recipe for a cheeseburger.

Bottom cheeseburger text

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Cheese
  • Hamburger buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Form the ground beef into a patty
  2. Grill it on for around 2 to 12 hours
  3. Apply as much ketchup as humanly possible
  4. Then add five times that in mustard
  5. Enjoy, you deserve it
